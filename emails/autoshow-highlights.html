Twin Cities Auto Show

Your Show Guide

Discover what’s waiting for you January 3–11, 2026.

Special exhibits and more

Explore classic cars, bold concept vehicles, and exclusive displays. Connect with industry experts, attend presentations, and hear how design and technology are shaping the future of transportation.

Try the Ride

Take out the vehicles you want—any brand, any style. Drive real city streets with a brand ambassador in the passenger seat to highlight performance, technology, safety, and everything that matters to you.

More variety, more driving, and the confidence that comes from comparing models back-to-back.

Learn more: Try the Ride overview

Fun for the whole family

Interactive exhibits, activities for kids, and photo-worthy displays make the show an all-day experience. Keep an eye out for classic cars, concept vehicles, and immersive brand zones.

Hundreds of new vehicles

Explore 25+ domestic and imported makes, including EVs and new technology showcases. Manufacturer reps are on-site to answer every question.

Easy to plan

Review features and vehicles ahead of time, then map your route on-site. Plan your ride-and-drives first—registration closes 30–60 minutes before daily drive hours end.

Buy tickets online

Group pricing: parties of 20+ receive $10 tickets. Download the order form here.
