Twin Cities Auto Show

Save the Date

January 3–11, 2026 · Minneapolis Convention Center

The Twin Cities Auto Show returns with hundreds of new cars, trucks, SUVs, and EVs under one roof. Explore concept vehicles, electric showcases, and special exhibits while industry experts share where transportation is headed next.

Try the Ride: more choices, more driving

Test drive the vehicles you actually want, side-by-side. Dealers can now showcase any make, so you can compare gas, hybrid, and EV models on real city streets with a brand ambassador riding along to point out performance and tech.

Move from browsing to confident—this is the best way to feel the difference before you buy.
